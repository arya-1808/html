Jal Mahal,Rajasthan

Jal Mahal (the "Water Palace") is a stunning architectural marvel located in
the middle of Man Sagar Lake in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Originally built
around 1699 and later expanded in the 18th century, the red sandstone palace
is a breathtaking blendof Rajput and Mughal styles, famous for appearing
to float on the water

Jaipur,Rajasthan

Jaipur, the capital and largest city of Rajasthan, is world-famous as the "Pink City".
Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II and designed by Vidyadhar Bhattacharya,
it is India’s first planned city. Jaipur anchors the famous Golden Triangle tourist circuit
alongside Delhi and Agra
