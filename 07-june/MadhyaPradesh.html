Jahaz Mahal,Madhya Pradesh

Jahaz Mahal, or the Ship Palace, is a stunning 15th-century,
two-story structure in Mandu, Madhya Pradesh. Built by
Sultan Ghiyas-ud-din Khilji to house his royal harem, it is
uniquely situated between Munj Talao and Kapur Talao. Its
reflection in the surrounding waters gives the breathtaking
illusion of a floating ship.

Ujjain,Madhya Pradesh

Ujjain is an ancient and deeply spiritual city located on
the banks of the sacred Kshipra River in the central Indian
state of Madhya Pradesh. It is globally renowned as one of the
seven sacred cities of Hinduism (Sapta Puri) and serves as a
major pilgrimage hub hosting the historic Simhastha Kumbh Mela
every 12 years. Historically called Ujjayini and Avanti, it was
a prominent center of learning, astronomy, and political power under
legendary rulers like King Vikramaditya.
